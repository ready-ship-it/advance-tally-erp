# HSN Research for Electronics and Computers

## Common HSN Codes for Electronics
- **8471**: Automatic data processing machines and units thereof (Computers, Laptops).
- **8473**: Parts and accessories (other than covers, carrying cases and the like) suitable for use solely or principally with machines of headings 8470 to 8472 (Processors, Motherboards).
- **8501**: Electric motors and generators.
- **8517**: Telephone sets, including smartphones and other voice/image/data transmission equipment.
- **8528**: Monitors and projectors, not incorporating television reception apparatus; reception apparatus for television.
- **8523**: Discs, tapes, solid-state non-volatile storage devices, "smart cards" and other media for the recording of sound or of other phenomena (SSD, Pendrives).
- **8544**: Insulated wire, cable and other insulated electric conductors (Cables).
- **8415**: Air-conditioners (Khosla Electronics common item).
